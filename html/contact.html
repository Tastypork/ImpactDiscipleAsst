About

Impact Recap is an independent tool designed to help attendees of Impact Church engage more deeply with the sermons. While we are not affiliated with Impact Church, our goal is to make it easier for you to understand the key messages and apply them to your everyday life. Impact Recap is here to assist you in reflecting on each sermon's themes, ideas, and values so you can grow spiritually and integrate faith-based teachings into practical, daily actions. If you have any questions, feedback, or would like to report a bug please use the form on the left. God Bless!
